History

Amelia Herrera opened her first restaurant on Maple Avenue in 1971, with just nine tables and a dream of leaving each one of her children with a restaurant of their own.

A new generation of a Dallas Tex-Mex staple, Cafe Herrera continues the tradition of the Herrera family, fusing heritage with contemporary Mexican food & classic cocktails.
